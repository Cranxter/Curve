Adnan Ahmad

I currently work as a Jr Data Scientist at DeepTek Inc and a student researcher at Symbiosis Center for Medical Image Analysis.

It is said that always let your problem define your solutions, so that is how my interest in Deep Learning first materialised back in 2015 as I searched through ways to enable better content discovery in my two most used applications - YouTube (music) and Guardian (news).

Since then that interest has further lead me to take a deep dive in working on research problems at the intersection of Machine Learning and Medical Imaging with my first research paper getting published at ISBI 2019.

Additionally, I have also taught practical Deep Learning at Technical Workshops in Pune to participants from diverse backgrounds.

This blog serves to chronicle my Deep Learning adventures.
